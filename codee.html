About Clement

Celebrating a beautiful life

CLEMENT was a calm, humble, and remarkable young man whose life reflected discipline, love, and kindness. He was admired for his gentle nature, his passion for learning, and his deep respect for others. From an early age, he showed a love for knowledge and personal growth. He began his educational journey at Staff Nursery/Primary School, Ogoli-Ugboju, where he obtained his Primary School Certificate (1992–1998). He continued to build a strong academic foundation at Government Commercial Secondary School, Atlo, earning his Senior School Certificate (2000–2006).

His dedication and love for science led him to the University of Mkar Gboko, Benue State, where he graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Microbiology (2011–2015). During his time there, he was known for his focus, humility, and his desire to make a positive difference in the lives of others. Beyond his academic accomplishments, he was a man of simplicity and class — always neat, organized, and full of calm confidence. He loved cleanliness and believed it reflected one's inner peace and self-respect. His major passion was to study MEDICINE and become a medical doctor. Friends and family knew him as cool, down to earth, and genuine in every sense. He was also computer literate, loved reading, had strong communication skills, and enjoyed watching movies in his leisure time.

Though he is no longer with us, his memory continues to live in our hearts. He will always be remembered for his quiet strength, his kindness, and his unwavering humility. "The memory of the righteous is blessed." – Proverbs 10:7 May his gentle soul rest in perfect peace. 🕊
